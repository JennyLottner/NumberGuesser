How does this work?

This process is based on a concept called binary search, where the range of possible numbers is continually halved with each guess. By the time it makes 7 guesses, it will have narrowed down the range enough to find the correct number!

Why 7 Guesses?

The binary search method allows the Number Guesser to narrow down a range of numbers quickly. With each guess, the search range is divided in half, and it takes at most 7 guesses to guess the correct number in the range from 0 to 100. This is because:

2^7 = 128, which is just over 100, so 7 guesses are enough to cover all possibilities!
